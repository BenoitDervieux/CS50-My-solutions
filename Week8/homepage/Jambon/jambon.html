The one and only recipe of the Jambon-beurre

Discover the one and only recipe of the most famous French sandwhich a.k.a. the Jambon Beurre

The recipe

You'll need:

  • Bread - (Une bonne baguette)
  • Ham - (Sans nitrates bien sûr)
  • Butter - (Du vrai fait localement)
  • Cheese - (Y'en a de la team comté mais emmental ça va)

How to do?

  1. Cut your baguette in 2 parts following the length of course
  2. Put butter on one side
  3. Put cheese on the other side
  4. And then you know how to make a sandwich isn't it? Good.
